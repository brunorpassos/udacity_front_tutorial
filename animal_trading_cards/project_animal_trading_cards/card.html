Koala

Koalas are largely sedentary and sleep up to 20 hours a day.

The koala is a stocky animal with a large head and vestigial or non-existent tail.The pelage of the koala is thicker and longer on the back, and shorter on the belly. The ears have thick fur on both the inside and outside. The back fur colour varies from light grey to chocolate brown. The belly fur is whitish; on the rump it is dappled whitish, and darker at the back.
